📖 How to Use

1

Start XKG - Run XKG on your computer: python main.py in the x-knowledge-graph folder.

2

Configure - Open XKG Mobile, go to Settings, enter your XKG endpoint.

3

Launch - Tap any AI chatbot to open it in your phone's browser.

4

Search - Use the search bar to search your XKG knowledge base.

⚠️ For local network access, your phone and computer must be on the same WiFi, and you must use your computer's IP address (e.g., http://192.168.1.100:5000).
